Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this towering minaret is located in Delhi and showcases Indo-Islamic architecture.

Hampi

Hampi is an ancient village in Karnataka, known for its magnificent temples and ruins from the Vijayanagara Empire.

Amber Fort

Located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, Amber Fort is a majestic blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture.
